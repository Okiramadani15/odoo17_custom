Oki Addons

Custom module for business

Features:

  • Customized Sales Order with Division
  • Customer Credit Limit per Division
  • Stock Inventory Report

Sales Order Customization:

  • Division field (SQA, GLN, BTV, BVG)
  • Customer filtering based on division
  • Pickup Method (Delivery or Take in Plant)
  • Credit limit warning
  • Overdue invoice warning

Stock Inventory Report:

  • Initial quantity
  • In quantity
  • Out quantity
  • Final quantity
  • Filtering by date range and location
